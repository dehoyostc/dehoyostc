# Hi, I'm T.C. 👋

I'm a Creative Director and Product Designer exploring the future of trustworthy AI communication.

I believe the next generation of AI won't simply answer questions—it will act as a trusted representative on behalf of people and organizations. That requires a new foundation for identity, trust, privacy, and interoperability.

## What I'm Building

### ACP — Agent Communication Protocol
An open protocol for structured, privacy-preserving communication between AI agents acting on behalf of people and organizations.

### CARP — Confidence-Aware Representation Protocol
The representation layer within ACP, enabling AI systems to exchange confidence-weighted, provenance-aware claims instead of opaque assertions.

### Jerry
A personal AI representative capable of recovering trusted profiles, reconciling knowledge across AI systems, evaluating compatibility, detecting divergences, and negotiating permissioned information disclosure.
